Konasal Training Institute

Who We Are

Konasal Training Institute is a premier career-focused education provider dedicated to helping students launch successful careers in technology and beyond. Founded in 2010, we've helped over 10,000 graduates transition into rewarding careers.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing industry-relevant training that equips students with the skills employers demand. We believe in practical, hands-on learning that prepares students for real-world challenges.

With 15+ years of excellence in career education, we offer industry-recognized certification programs through a hands-on, project-based learning approach. We maintain strong partnerships with leading employers to ensure our graduates have the best job opportunities.

Our modern campus features state-of-the-art labs and learning facilities, with small class sizes that ensure personalized attention for every student.
